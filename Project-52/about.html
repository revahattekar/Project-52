Hi Everyone. My name is Honey. I am a 21yr old college student and the owner of the ORIGAMI DESIGN'S. Me and my team try to provide you with the best origami designs and proper instructions on how to make them. Step by Step diagrams are probably the most popular, easiest and the most followed way to show how to fold things out of paper. Trying to find good origami instructions on the Internet can be a lot of work though. To help your search we’ve put together the largest database of free origami diagrams anywhere on the Internet.
